## 2. Blood Relations
Use standard notation to draw family trees quickly:
- **Square [ ] / Plus (+)** = Male member
- **Circle ( ) / Minus (-)** = Female member
- **Double Horizontal Line (=)** = Married Couple (Husband = Wife)
- **Single Horizontal Line (-)** = Siblings (Brother - Sister)
- **Vertical Line (|)** = Generation gap (Parent to Child)

## 3. Clocks
- **Angle between Hour and Minute hands:**
  $$\theta = \left|30H - \frac{11}{2}M\right|$$
  *(where $H = \text{Hour}$, $M = \text{Minutes}$)*
- **Hand Speeds:**
  - Minute Hand speed $= 6^\circ$ per minute.
  - Hour Hand speed $= 0.5^\circ$ per minute.
  - Relative Speed $= 5.5^\circ$ per minute.
- **Coincidence / Straight Line / Right Angle:**
  - Hands coincide ($0^\circ$) $\rightarrow$ 22 times in 24 hours (once every $65\frac{5}{11}$ mins).
  - Hands are opposite ($180^\circ$) $\rightarrow$ 22 times in 24 hours.
  - Hands are at right angle ($90^\circ$) $\rightarrow$ 44 times in 24 hours.

## 4. Calendars
- **Odd Days Concept:**
  - Standard Year (365 days) $= 52 \text{ weeks} + 1 \text{ odd day}$.
  - Leap Year (366 days) $= 52 \text{ weeks} + 2 \text{ odd days}$.
- **Odd Days in Centuries:**
  - 100 Years $= 5$ odd days
  - 200 Years $= 3$ odd days
  - 300 Years $= 1$ odd day
  - 400 Years $= 0$ odd days (e.g., 400, 800, 1200, 1600, 2000, 2400)
- **Leap Year Rule:**
  - Year divisible by 4 (e.g., 2024) is a leap year.
  - Century year (ending in 00) must be divisible by 400 (e.g., 1600 is leap, but 1700, 1800, 1900 are not).

## 5. Syllogisms (Standard Statements)
- **All A are B:** A is completely inside B.
- **Some A are B:** Overlapping region between A and B.
- **No A is B:** A and B are completely separate with a cross-line.
- **Some A are not B:** A part of A cannot enter B.
- **Complementary Pairs (Either-Or conditions):**
  1. (Some + No)
  2. (All + Some Not)
  *(Subject and Predicate must be the same in both conclusions, and both conclusions must be individually false/doubtful).*
